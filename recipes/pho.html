Pho (Vietnamese Soup)

Description

This delicous noodle soup is very common. With love and hard work the final product will come out amazing.

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minute or more!

Ingredients

  • Beef broth
  • Yello onion
  • Fresh ginger
  • Ground coriander, cloves, fish sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sause, red chili paste, cinnamon, salt and pepper
  • 8 oz. dried vermicelli noodles
  • Pork or steak, brisket, round eye. any of these
  • Green onions
  • chili peppers
  • cilantro, basil leaves, mint leaves
  • bean sprout
  • Lime wedges
  • Sriacha

Instructions

  1. Get a large pot on medium heat and add onions and ginger for 4 minutes.

  2. Add broth, water, coriander, clove, fish sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, chili garlic paste, cinnamon stick and a pinch of fresh cracked salt and pepper to the pot. Bring to a slow boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or more.

  3. Prepare noodles according to Instructions on package.

  4. Add the meat to the broth and cook until not raw.

  5. Enjoy your pho!!!!!
